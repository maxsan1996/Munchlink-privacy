2. Scope of This Policy

This policy applies to all personal and personal health information collected through the MunchLink mobile application (iOS and Android) and any related services. It explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how it is stored and protected, and your rights as a user.

Important: MunchLink is a data tracking and pattern interpretation tool only. It does not provide medical diagnosis, clinical advice, or replace the judgment of a licensed physician or registered dietitian. All AI-generated summaries in the app include this disclaimer.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Account Information

  • Email address (used for authentication via Supabase Auth)
  • Password (hashed and never stored in plain text)
  • Account creation date and last sign-in timestamp

3.2 Child Profile Information

When you create a child profile, you provide:

  • Child’s first name or nickname (you may use an alias)
  • Date of birth (used to calculate age-appropriate nutritional baselines)
  • Gender (optional, used for clinical report context)

We do not collect OHIP numbers, health card numbers, or any government-issued identifiers.

3.3 Health Tracking Data

The following data is logged at your direction when you use the app:

  • Bowel movement logs: Bristol Stool Scale type, consistency, colour, notes, timestamp
  • Meal logs: food name, texture, colour, portion completion, timestamp
  • Hydration logs: beverage type, volume, clinical weight (if entered), timestamp
  • Sensory journey logs: food exposure steps, reaction scores, caregiver notes
  • Food chains: therapeutic food introduction plans, target foods, restriction levels
  • Daily gut health scores: composite score, confidence ratio, aggregated metrics

3.4 Media

If you use the camera-based meal logging feature, photos you take are sent to Google’s Gemini AI API for food identification and are not stored on our servers. See Section 7 for details on third-party processing.

3.5 Technical Data

  • Device type and operating system version (collected by Supabase for session management)
  • App crash reports (if enabled via Expo)
  • We do not collect advertising identifiers (IDFA), precise location, or contacts

6. Data Security

We implement the following technical safeguards to protect your personal health information:

  • Row Level Security (RLS) enforced at the database level: you can only access your own child’s data
  • All data transmitted over HTTPS / TLS
  • JWT-authenticated API access: every request is validated before data is returned
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt via Supabase Auth and never stored in plain text
  • Service role database credentials are never exposed to the client application
  • Supabase Edge Functions validate authentication on every invocation

No security system is 100% infallible. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal health information, we will notify affected users in accordance with PHIPA breach notification requirements.

8. Children’s Privacy

MunchLink is designed for use by parents and caregivers to track data about their minor children. We do not permit children to create their own accounts or directly input their own data.

  • The account holder must be the parent or legal guardian of the child
  • Data entered about a child is associated with the parent’s account and subject to their consent
  • We do not knowingly collect data directly from minors under 13

If you believe a minor has created an account without parental consent, contact us at maxime.sandjian@gmail.com and we will delete the account promptly.

9. Your Rights Under PHIPA

As a user and the parent or guardian of the child whose data is tracked, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal health information at any time via the app or by contacting us
  • Right to correct inaccurate information by editing records directly in the app
  • Right to withdraw consent and delete your account and all associated data (see Section 10)
  • Right to data portability — you can export your child’s clinical report as a PDF at any time from within the app
  • Right to lodge a complaint with the Information and Privacy Commissioner of Ontario (IPC) if you believe your rights have been violated

To exercise any of these rights, email us at maxime.sandjian@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Account & Data Deletion

You may delete your account and all associated personal health information at any time. Account deletion is available directly within the app under Settings → Account → Delete Account.

Upon deletion:

  • All child profile data, health logs, and gut scores are permanently deleted from our database
  • Your authentication credentials are removed from Supabase Auth
  • Deletion is irreversible. We do not retain backups of deleted accounts beyond 30 days
Apple App Store requirement: Account deletion is available in-app, not just by email request. This satisfies Apple’s App Store Review Guidelines §5.1.1 (Data Collection and Storage).

11. Data Retention

We retain your personal health information for as long as your account is active. If you do not delete your account, we do not automatically purge your data, as historical tracking data is core to the app’s value.

If you have not logged into your account for 24 consecutive months, we will send you a retention notice email. If you do not respond within 30 days, your account and data will be scheduled for deletion.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you via a push notification or in-app banner at least 14 days before the changes take effect. The updated policy will also be posted at the URL registered with the App Store.

Continued use of MunchLink after the effective date of changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
